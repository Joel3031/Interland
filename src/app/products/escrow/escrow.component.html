Escrow Architecture

Novus's Real Estate Escrow Account operates as an advanced, specialized banking infrastructure engineered specifically for Real Estate Developers. It ensures that every fragment of project capital is securely ring-fenced and orchestrated in strict accordance with the WAFI Off-Plan Sales and Rent Program.

Moving beyond simple storage, this platform acts as a complete financial command center. It guarantees absolute financial transparency—equipping both developers and buyers with the tools to track, manage, and audit project funds via an integrated, real-time dashboard.

What is a Real Estate Escrow Account?

A dedicated, secure bank account used exclusively for property development. All off-plan buyer payments are deposited here and rigidly protected. Funds are disbursed to the developer only when stringent, predefined project conditions are met.

Process

How It Works

A streamlined, five-step process for secure real estate development funding. Our platform takes complexity out of compliance, automating the flow of capital from buyer to developer securely.

1. Onboarding

Existing Riyad Bank customers can onboard using their CIS number. A new CIS is generated for the real estate project, under which the Escrow Account is created.

2. Project Setup

Developers create real estate units within the system. Each unit is assigned a unique Virtual IBAN.

3. Buyer Payments

Buyers make payments directly to the assigned Virtual IBAN. Payments are securely credited to the escrow account.

4. Fund Allocation

All incoming funds are automatically distributed into three sub-accounts as per WAFI rules.

5. Fund Release

Developers request fund disbursement by submitting required documents.

Structure

Smart Account Structure

To ensure absolute financial security and strict adherence to regulatory standards, each Escrow Account includes three regulated sub-accounts. Following the rigorous compliance guidelines established by WAFI rules, all incoming project funds are automatically distributed. Specifically, the capital is meticulously partitioned with 76% dedicated to the Construction Account, 20% allocated to the Marketing Account, and the remaining 4% securely held in the Retention Account. This highly structured approach guarantees proper fund utilization and compliance, ensuring operational liquidity and complete peace of mind for developers and investors.
