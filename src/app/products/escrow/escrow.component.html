Escrow Architecture

Real Estate Escrow Account operates as an advanced, specialized banking infrastructure engineered specifically for Real Estate Developers. It ensures that every fragment of project capital is securely ring-fenced and orchestrated in strict accordance with the WAFI Off-Plan Sales and Rent Program.

Moving beyond simple storage, this platform acts as a complete financial command center. It guarantees absolute financial transparency—equipping both developers and buyers with the tools to track, manage, and audit project funds via an integrated, real-time dashboard.

What is a Real Estate Escrow Account?

A dedicated, secure bank account used exclusively for property development. All off-plan buyer payments are deposited here and rigidly protected. Funds are disbursed to the developer only when stringent, predefined project conditions are met.

Process

How It Works

A streamlined, five-step process for secure real estate development funding. Our platform takes complexity out of compliance, automating the flow of capital from buyer to developer securely.

1. Onboarding

Existing corporate clients initiate onboarding via their primary CIS identifier. The platform then provisions a dedicated project-specific CIS, under which the regulatory Escrow Account is securely established.

2. Project Setup

Property developers configure individual real estate units directly inside the management console. The system programmatically allocates a dedicated, unique Virtual IBAN to every distinct unit.

3. Buyer Payments

Purchasers route their installment payments directly to the designated Virtual IBAN. Incoming tranches are immediately cleared and safely deposited into the master escrow ledger.

4. Fund Allocation

The intelligent routing mechanism autonomously fractions and splits all collected capital across three regulated sub-ledgers, enforcing strict alignment with WAFI guidelines.

5. Fund Release

To trigger milestone disbursements, developers upload the necessary evidentiary documentation and certificates to clear predefined regulatory checkpoints for capital release.

Structure

Smart Account Structure

To ensure absolute financial security and strict adherence to regulatory standards, each Escrow Account includes three regulated sub-accounts. Following the rigorous compliance guidelines established by WAFI rules, all incoming project funds are automatically distributed. Specifically, the capital is meticulously partitioned across the Construction Account, the Marketing Account, and the Retention Account according to mandated statutory ratios. This highly structured approach guarantees proper fund utilization and compliance, ensuring operational liquidity and complete peace of mind for developers and investors
